Features

Everything you need
on the road

Built from the saddle up by riders who've logged thousands of miles and wanted a single app that does it all.

Adaptive Training Plans

Cycly learns your fitness level, goals, and calendar. Training blocks adapt week by week — no generic 12-week plan you'll abandon by week three.

Elevation profile+740m

Route Discovery

Find new roads and trails near you. Filter by distance, elevation, surface type, and difficulty. Every route is rider-verified.

Clubs & Group Rides

Join local clubs, organise group rides, and share segments with your crew. Cycling is better together.

Performance Insights

Power, cadence, heart rate, VO2 trends — all in one dashboard. Know exactly what's working and what isn't.

Safety Features

Live location sharing, emergency contacts, and crash detection — because peace of mind matters on every ride.
